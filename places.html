Discover Places in Kosovo

Kosovo is a country of diverse landscapes and rich history. From vibrant cities and historic towns to mountains, canyons, and cultural heritage sites, Kosovo offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Prishtina

The capital city of Kosovo, known for its vibrant culture, modern lifestyle, and historical landmarks.

→

Prizren

A historic city famous for its old town, stone bridge, cultural festivals, and rich heritage.

→

Peja

Gateway to the Rugova mountains, offering nature, adventure tourism, and cultural sites.

→

Brezovica

Kosovo’s main ski resort, popular for winter sports and mountain tourism.

→

Rugova Canyon

A spectacular natural canyon known for hiking, climbing, and breathtaking landscapes.

→

Mitrovica

A city rich in history and culture, located in the northern part of Kosovo.

→

Bajgora

A mountainous region known for natural beauty, traditions, and eco-tourism.
