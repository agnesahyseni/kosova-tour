Discover Places in Kosovo

Kosovo is a country of diverse landscapes and rich history. From vibrant cities and historic towns to mountains, canyons, and cultural heritage sites, Kosovo offers unforgettable experiences.

Prishtina

The capital city of Kosovo, known for its vibrant culture and modern lifestyle.

Prizren

A historic city famous for its old town, stone bridge, and rich heritage.

Peja

A charming city in western Kosovo, known for its beautiful nature.

Brezovica

Kosovo’s main ski resort, popular for winter sports and mountain tourism.

Rugova Canyon

The place where adventure begins. Amazing views and fresh air.

Mitrovica

A city rich in history and culture, located in the northern part of Kosovo.

Bajgora

A mountainous region known for traditions and eco-tourism.

Explore Prishtina

Prishtina is the administrative and cultural heart of Kosovo, known for its youthful energy and endless coffee shops.

  • Cultural Landmarks: Visit the National Library of Kosovo, famous for its unique "brutalist" architecture.
  • City Life: Walk through Mother Teresa Boulevard to experience the vibrant pulse of the city.
  • History: See the NEWBORN monument, a symbol of the country's independence and creativity.
Historic Prizren

Often called the museum city, Prizren is a treasure trove of Ottoman-era architecture and historical significance.

  • The Fortress: Hike up to the Kalaja for a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire city.
  • Old Stone Bridge: A beautiful spot for photography crossing the Bistrica river.
  • Events: Home to Dokufest, an international documentary film festival held every summer.
Peja - City of Nature

Peja serves as the gateway to the Accursed Mountains and offers some of the best outdoor activities in the region.

  • Spiritual Heritage: Explore the Patriarchate of Peja, a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Bazar: Visit the Old Bazar of Peja for traditional crafts and authentic local food.
  • Adventure: The starting point for the famous "Peaks of the Balkans" hiking trail.
Brezovica Skiing

Located in the Sharr Mountains, Brezovica is the premier destination for winter sports lovers in Kosovo.

  • Winter Sports: Famous for its wide ski slopes and off-piste skiing opportunities.
  • Scenery: Even in summer, it offers fresh air and stunning mountain landscapes.
  • Tourism: A perfect spot for weekend getaways and mountain resort experiences.
Rugova Adventure

One of the longest and deepest canyons in Europe, Rugova is a masterpiece of natural erosion and beauty.

  • Extreme Sports: Try the Zip Line or rock climbing on the Via Ferrata.
  • Lakes: Discover the high-altitude Liqenat lakes surrounded by pine forests.
  • Caves: Home to many unexplored caves and natural water springs.
Cultural Mitrovica

A city known for its mining heritage and its symbolic bridge, Mitrovica has a unique place in Kosovo's identity.

  • Mining History: Explore the rich history of the Trepça mine, once one of the largest in Europe.
  • The Bridge: Visit the famous bridge over the Ibër River, a site of significant history.
  • Arts: A city with a strong rock music tradition and many talented local artists.
Bajgora Highlands

Shala e Bajgorës is a region characterized by rolling hills, fresh air, and a strong sense of tradition.

  • Eco-Tourism: Ideal for picnics, long walks, and enjoying organic mountain food.
  • Wind Energy: Home to Kosovo's wind turbine park, blending technology with nature.
  • Traditions: Experience the authentic life of the local highlands and their hospitality.
