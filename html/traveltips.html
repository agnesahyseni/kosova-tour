Travel Tips

Everything you need to know for an unforgettable experience in Kosovo.

Currency

Kosovo uses the Euro (€). While cards are widely accepted in cities, it’s recommended to carry cash for small shops and rural areas.

Safety

Kosovo is one of the friendliest countries in Europe. People are very helpful and many speak English and German well.

Transport

Buses are the main mode of transport. They are affordable and connect all major cities. In cities like Prishtina, taxis or local ride-hailing apps are convenient.

Best Time to Visit

April to October is ideal. The mountains are perfect for hiking in summer, while Brezovica and Bogaj are popular for skiing in winter.

Pro Tip

Don't just stop at the tourist spots! Go to an "Oxhak" or old coffee shop in Prizren or Peja and ask for a macchiato. Kosovo is known for its coffee culture that rivals Italy - it's where all the social life happens!
